<objective>
Move all business logic, audit wiring, and database interactions (`db.add`, `db.flush`, `db.commit`) out of the FastAPI `decisions` router into a dedicated `DecisionService` to strictly adhere to Clean Architecture.

Purpose: Achieve total decoupling of HTTP concerns from business/DB concerns to facilitate testing and adhere to the Single Responsibility Principle.
Output: A new service class `DecisionService` and a refactored, anemic router for `/decisions`.
</objective>
