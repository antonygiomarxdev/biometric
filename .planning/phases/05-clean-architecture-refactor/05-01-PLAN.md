<objective>
Move all business logic and database interactions (`db.add`, `db.commit`, etc.) out of the FastAPI routers for Cases and Evidence into dedicated Service classes to strictly adhere to Clean Architecture.

Purpose: Achieve total decoupling of HTTP concerns from business/DB concerns to facilitate testing and adhere to the Single Responsibility Principle.
Output: Two new service classes (`CaseService` and `EvidenceService`) and refactored, anemic routers for `/cases` and `/evidence`.
</objective>
